Travel Tips & Essentials

Entry requirements (Ukrainians)

  • Valid biometric passport — mandatory
  • No visa needed for up to 90 days in the Republic
  • Travel health insurance required
  • Return / onward ticket proof required at border
  • Northern Ireland = UK visa required — itinerary avoids it entirely

Practical tips

  • May weather: expect 10–16 °C, sunny spells and rain showers — bring a waterproof layer
  • Cash is useful for small pubs and market stalls; cards accepted almost everywhere
  • Heritage Ireland Card (€40 per person) covers Rock of Cashel, Kilkenny Castle, Ross Castle & more — great value for this itinerary
  • Fuel: petrol ~€1.75/L — budget ~€80–100 in fuel for the full loop
  • Phone data: EU roaming applies if you have an EU SIM; otherwise buy an Irish SIM at Dublin Airport
  • Tipping: ~10–15% in restaurants; not obligatory in pubs
